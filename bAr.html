Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up to ride with Transprio?

Click the "Apply Now" button above, fill out our online application form and submit the required documents. Once you receive approval and complete our rider onboarding process, you’ll be ready to start earning good money on your own schedule!

Do I need a delivery bike to be able to ride with Transprio?

Yes, but if you don’t own a bike, you need not worry — there are other options available. Our partners can supply you a bike for competitive rate which is accepted by us.

Do I need a smartphone to ride with Transprio?

You should have a smartphone that runs at least Android 9.0 or iOS 14.

Is riding with Transprio a good side hustle?

The advantage of riding with Transprio is that you can work based on your own schedule — which makes it the perfect side hustle. There are no minimum hours, drive all week or just in the evenings or at weekends. Go online whenever you want.

How do I get paid for riding with Transprio?

Our payment cycle runs from Monday 00:00 to the following Sunday at 23:59, after which you’ll receive a balance statement. If you’re due a payout, you’ll receive it on a weekly basis — no need to wait until the end of the month.
