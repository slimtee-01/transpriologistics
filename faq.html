Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

How are fares calculated?

Fares are calculated per distance and per time by the app. With the base fare of 100 naira, fare estimate is calculated thus: 100 naira base fare + 40 naira per kilometer + 15 naira per minute. We charge 500 naira as the minimum fare.

What are the payment options?

We have two options. You can choose to either pay by cash or by using your debit card in the app.

My fare does not reflect the upfront price i was shown.

Report such cases to support on the app with a screenshot of the affected order.

How do I get a copy of the receipt?

Go to “Deliveries” on the app, select the order you need receipt for, then select “Export Deliveries”.

Can I cancel my order?

From your delivery page on the app, you can cancel any order. Simply spot the order on the delivery page and choose the cancel option. Ensure you are cancelling both the pick up and drop off tasks.

Am I charged for cancelling?

Currently we do not charge a cancellation fee. However, we encourage cancellations only when there is a valid reason. Excessive cancellations might lead to blocking of the user.

Do your riders handle 'Payment on Delivery?

About Joining Transprio

I have all of the qualifications listed, but I wasn’t offered an interview. Why?

Not receiving an interview despite having all the qualifications could be due to factors like high competition, resume presentation, networking, timing, application errors, company preferences, or hidden requirements. Seeking feedback, networking, and persistence can improve your chances.

I just submitted my application, what can I expect?

After submitting your application, you can expect the hiring process to unfold. This typically includes an initial review of applications, potential interviews, and if successful, further stages like assessments or follow-up interviews. It's crucial to stay patient and proactive during this waiting period.

If I’m turned down, will you provide me with feedback so that I can improve?

Certainly! Many employers provide feedback to candidates who were not selected. You can politely ask for feedback after being turned down to gain insights and improve your future applications.

What is the interview process like at Transprio?
  • Application Submission: Start by submitting your application and resume through their website or a job portal.
  • Resume Screening: The company's HR team or recruiters will review applications to shortlist candidates who meet the basic qualifications.
  • Initial Interview: This might be a phone or video interview with HR to discuss your background, skills, and interest in the position.
  • Technical/Behavioral Interviews: Depending on the role, you might have one or more interviews with team members or hiring managers. These could cover technical skills, behavioral questions, or both.
  • Assessments: Some positions may require skills assessments, tests, or presentations to evaluate your abilities.
  • Reference Checks: The company might contact your provided references to verify your qualifications and work history.
  • Final Interview: You may have a final interview with senior management or executives to assess your fit within the company.
  • Offer: If you're successful, they'll extend a job offer, which includes details about compensation, benefits, and other terms.
  • Onboarding: Once you accept the offer, the onboarding process begins, where you'll complete paperwork and get acquainted with the company.

    • Please note that specific interview processes can vary widely depending on the role, company policies, and industry. It's always a good idea to research the company and reach out to their HR department or website for the most accurate and up-to-date information on their interview process.

Become a rider with Transprio

How do I sign up to ride with Transprio?

Click on Become a Transprio rider page then navigate to "Apply Now" button by clicking the Apply button at the top of the page, fill out our online application form and submit the required documents. Once you receive approval and complete our rider onboarding process, you’ll be ready to start earning good money on your own schedule!

Do I need a delivery bike to be able to ride with Transprio?

Yes, but if you don’t own a bike, you need not worry — there are other options available. Our partners can supply you a bike for competitive rate which is accepted by us.

Do I need a smartphone to ride with Transprio?

You should have a smartphone that runs at least Android 9.0 or iOS 14.

Is riding with Transprio a good side hustle?

The advantage of riding with Transprio is that you can work based on your own schedule — which makes it the perfect side hustle. There are no minimum hours, drive all week or just in the evenings or at weekends. Go online whenever you want.

How do I get paid for riding with Transprio?

Our payment cycle runs from Monday 00:00 to the following Sunday at 23:59, after which you’ll receive a balance statement. If you’re due a payout, you’ll receive it on a weekly basis — no need to wait until the end of the month.

Common Issues

My pickup or drop off was wrong

In case you have informed a wrong drop-off address when placing your order, you can contact the pilot informing the correct address. Please note that delivery charges may change accordingly to the new drop-off address.

How do i prepare my package for pickup

Different packages require different preparation methods. If you are delivering a meal, kindly seal it properly. If you’re delivering items that can be broken when pressure is applied, we advise that you secure it further with a bubble wrap.

We suggest you provide us further details in the notes section of the order. Alternatively, you can speak to your designated pilot and explain the fragility of your product so as to enable them take safety precautions

Can my rider as for tips?

Pilots are not allowed to ask for tips, but you can willingly give.

My rider made away with my package.

We apologise for this inconvenience. Be assured that our pilots are constantly tracked by Transprio through GPS and special trackers installed on the bike.

Kindly contact the support team with the details of the trip and we’ll take it up from there.

My rider is not responding.

Kindly contact the support team with the details of the trip and we’ll take it up from there.
