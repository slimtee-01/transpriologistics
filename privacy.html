Privacy Policy for Transprio Users

At Transprio, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal data when you use our logistics app (Transprio). By accessing or using our App, you hereby agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information: We may collect certain personal information such as your name, contact information (including email address, phone number), delivery address, and payment details (credit card information, bank details) when you sign up, book logistics services, or make payments through our App.

1.2 Device Information: We also collect information about the device you use to access our App, including the device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, IP address, and mobile network information.

1.3 Location Information: Our App requires access to your location to provide accurate pickup and delivery services. With your consent, we collect information about your precise location through GPS, Wi-Fi, or cellular networks.

2. Use of Information

2.1 Delivery Services: We use your personal information to facilitate pickup and delivery services, coordinate transportation, communicate with drivers, and complete your requested deliveries efficiently.

2.2 Account Management: We use your personal information to create and manage your account, to communicate with you about your account, provide customer support, and address any technical or logistical issues.

2.3 Marketing and Promotions: With your consent, we may use your personal information to send you promotional communications, newsletters, and updates about our services, discounts, or special offers that may be of interest to you. You can withdraw your consent at any time.

2.4 Analytics and Improvements: We may use the collected data to analyze usage patterns, conduct research, improve our App’s features, and enhance our services' overall performance and functionality.

3. Sharing of Information

3.1 Service Providers: We may disclose your personal information to third-party service providers, contractors, or agents who assist us in providing logistics services, processing payments, or helping us analyze our App’s usage.

3.2 Compliance with Laws: We may share your personal information when required to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or enforceable governmental requests.

3.3 Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our business or assets, your personal information may be transferred to the successor entity.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information and maintain its confidentiality. However, please note that no electronic transmission or storage method is completely secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, and as required by applicable laws.

6. Your Rights

You have certain rights related to your personal information, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict its processing. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@transprio.com.

7. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our website, and you will be deemed to have accepted the revised Privacy Policy on your next App access.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at support@transprio.com
